# Writing your mail

See also: [reading](reading-mail.md) · [organizing](organizing-mail.md) ·
[accounts](accounts.md).

## A new message

Choose **New message** on the left. Fill in:

- **To** — one or more addresses, separated by commas. As you type, GeleitMail suggests addresses
  you've corresponded with; click a suggestion to fill it in.
- **Cc** — optional; also with address suggestions.
- **Subject** and the **message body**.

Then choose **Send**. GeleitMail sends through your account's outgoing (SMTP) server (set up when you
added the account) and saves a copy to your **Sent** folder. Sending happens in the background, so
the app stays responsive; if something goes wrong you get a short, plain explanation and your draft
stays open to try again.

## Replying and forwarding

Open a message and use the actions at the top of the reading area:

- **Reply** — writes back to the sender, quoting the original.
- **Reply all** — also includes everyone who was on the original (minus your own address).
- **Forward** — sends the message on to someone new.

Replies keep the conversation threaded, and the **from** address is automatically the account you're
reading in.

## Attachments

In the compose window, choose **Browse…** to pick a file with your system's file chooser (or type a
path), then **Attach**. Attached files are listed; remove one with **✕**. Attachments are included
when you send — and they're kept if you save the message as a draft and come back to it later.

## Formatting (Markdown)

Turn on **Format with Markdown** to write with light formatting: `**bold**`, `*italics*`, `- lists`,
`[links](https://example.com)`, and so on. The message goes out so it reads correctly both as
formatted mail and as plain text.

## Your signature

If you set a **Signature** when adding the account, it's appended to messages you compose, reply to,
and forward. Change it by reconnecting the account through the same form
(see [accounts](accounts.md)).

## Drafts

Not ready to send? Choose **Save draft** — the message (recipients, subject, body, and any
attachments) is saved on this device. Open **Drafts** on the left to see saved drafts and pick one to
**resume** editing. Sending a resumed draft removes it from the drafts list.
