# GeleitMail — User Manual

GeleitMail is a local-first, privacy-first email client.

- **[Reading your mail](reading-mail.md)** — sign in, the layout, opening messages, refresh, offline.
- **[Writing your mail](writing-mail.md)** — compose, reply/forward, Cc, recipient chips, attachments,
  signature.
- **[Organizing your mail](organizing-mail.md)** — archive, delete/trash, move, junk, and Undo.
- **[Searching your mail](searching-mail.md)** — instant offline search, operators, all accounts.
- **[Accounts](accounts.md)** — multiple accounts, switching, "All inboxes", the right sender,
  removing an account.
- **[Keyboard & settings](shortcuts-and-appearance.md)** — shortcuts, light/dark theme, the Settings
  window.
- **[Privacy & security](privacy.md)** — no telemetry, encryption at rest, blocked remote content.

> First release is **Linux**. One-click Gmail/Outlook (OAuth) and macOS/Windows are planned; today
> sign-in is manual IMAP/SMTP. See the project [`CHANGELOG`](../../CHANGELOG.md).
