# Accounts

See also: [reading](reading-mail.md) (first sign-in) · [searching across accounts](searching-mail.md).

## More than one account

GeleitMail can hold several accounts at once. Click your account name at the top of the left rail to
open the **account menu**, then **Add account** — the add-account wizard opens, and your current
account stays put while you fill it in.

## Switching accounts

The account menu lists every account. Click one to switch: the folders and messages shown become that
account's. Each account keeps its own folders, messages, and search index, separately.

## All inboxes

With more than one account, the account menu also offers **All inboxes** — one merged, newest-first
list of *every* account's inbox. Each row shows which account the message came from. While you're in
this view the per-account folders are hidden (folders belong to individual accounts); pick a specific
account from the menu to bring its folders back. Search and refresh work across all accounts here.

## The right sender, automatically

When you reply, reply-all, forward, or write a new message, the **from** address is the account you're
currently viewing — and in **All inboxes**, opening a message adopts the account that received it, so
your reply always goes out from the right address.

## Removing an account

Open **Settings → Accounts**, choose **Remove** next to the account, and confirm. This deletes the
local copy of that account's mail and its saved password from your system keychain. **Your mail stays
safe on the server** — removing it here only clears the copy on this device, and you can add the
account again any time to re-download it.
