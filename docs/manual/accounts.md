# Accounts

See also: [reading](reading-mail.md) (first sign-in) · [searching across accounts](searching-mail.md).

## More than one account

GeleitMail can hold several accounts at once. Under your account name on the left, choose
**+ Add account** to add another — the same form as the first sign-in opens, and your current account
stays put while you fill it in. **Cancel** backs out.

## Switching accounts

Your other accounts are listed under the current one in the left rail. Click one to switch: the
folders and messages shown become that account's. Each account keeps its own folders, messages, and
search index, separately.

## The right sender, automatically

When you reply, reply-all, forward, or write a new message, the **from** address is the account
you're currently viewing — so replies always go out from the right address.

## Removing an account

To make GeleitMail forget the account you're viewing, choose **Remove account** at the bottom of the
folder list and confirm. This deletes the local copy of that account's mail and its saved password
from your system keychain, then switches you to another account (or back to the setup form if it was
your last). **Your mail stays safe on the server** — removing it here only clears the copy on this
device, and you can add the account again any time to re-download it.
